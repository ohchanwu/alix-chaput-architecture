PASSION: DRAWING

Outside my architectural practice, and as an amateur, drawing holds an important place in my life. It is one of the fundamental reasons that led me to this profession, and I continue to practise it out of passion beyond the professional sphere.


I gladly foreground the works that represent me today, and I am also continually seeking to improve. Since learning and progress are intrinsically linked to any artistic practice, I constantly explore the world of drawing in search of both technical and creative discoveries. My personal practice sometimes meets the world of architecture when, as shown below, I paint scenes in which architecture dominates.


But it also moves widely away from it when my subjects take entirely different forms, such as objects or the human figure. The following selection is neither exhaustive nor fully representative of my universe, but it shows a range of subjects and techniques with which I am familiar and that correspond to me.
